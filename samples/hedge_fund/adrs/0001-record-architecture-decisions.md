# 1. Record architecture decisions

Date: 2026-05-04

## Status

Accepted

## Context

We need to record the architectural decisions made on the trading
platform so that new engineers understand why the estate looks the
way it does.

## Decision

We will use Architecture Decision Records, as described by Michael
Nygard, kept in `adrs/` next to the workspace model.

## Consequences

Decisions are versioned with the model and rendered in the
pystructurizr web app alongside the diagrams.
